Tindangle FTK – Duel Links

Isometric Inferno • High-risk First Turn Kill

Overview

This deck aims to achieve a full LP kill in a single turn using Tindangle monsters, Stairs of Mail, and a precise sequence of flips, links, and XYZ summons.

⚠ Important: This is a high-risk combo deck. If interrupted, you will almost always lose.

Deck Requirements

  • Must go first
  • No opponent LP gain or LP damage
  • No effect negation (Veiler, Spirit Dragon, etc.)
  • Correct starting hand distribution

Decklist

Main Deck

  • Tindangle Protector
  • Tindangle Intruder
  • Tindangle Jhrelth
  • Tindangle Delaunay
  • Tindangle Dholes
  • Needle Ball
  • Shaddoll Squamata
  • Resh Shaddoll Incarnation

Extra Deck

  • Number 47: Nightmare Shark
  • Number 6: Chronomaly Atlantis
  • Knightmare Unicorn
  • Subterror Behemoth Fiendess
  • Agave Dragon

FTK Combo – Step by Step

Click to expand combo steps
  1. Activate Isometric Inferno at start of turn.
  2. Send required Tindangle cards to the Graveyard.
  3. Set and activate Stairs of Mail.
  4. Trigger Tindangle Delaunay to summon face-down monsters.
  5. Flip Tindangle Protector and resolve draws.
  6. Chain flip effects of Jhrelth and Intruder.
  7. Link Summon Subterror Behemoth Fiendess.
  8. Send Shaddoll Squamata → Resh Shaddoll Incarnation.
  9. XYZ Summon Number 47.
  10. XYZ Summon Number 6 and halve opponent LP.
  11. Link Summon Agave Dragon and gain LP.
  12. Repeatedly flip Needle Ball to finish the duel.
✅ Result: Opponent reaches 0 LP during your first turn.

Why This Deck Isn’t Meta

  • Extremely fragile
  • Loses to any quick negation
  • LP changes instantly break the combo
  • Very strict sequencing
